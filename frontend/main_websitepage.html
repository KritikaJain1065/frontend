Indian Health Campaigns & Yojnas

  • Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

    👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For: Economically vulnerable families
    💰 Coverage: Up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year

    Provides health insurance coverage for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization. It aims to help economically vulnerable families and offers cashless and paperless access to healthcare services.

    How to Apply: Visit your nearest Ayushman Bharat Kendra, Common Service Centre, or register through the official PM-JAY portal.

  • National Health Mission (NHM)

    👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For: All citizens with focus on rural areas
    🏥 Type: Primary healthcare

    Aims to provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to rural areas with special focus on the states with weak public health indicators and infrastructure.

    Benefits: Improved healthcare facilities, reduced disease burden, and strengthened public health systems.

  • Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)

    🤰 For: Pregnant women
    📅 Services: Free checkups on 9th of every month

    Provides fixed-day assured, comprehensive and quality antenatal care to pregnant women on the 9th of every month to ensure safe pregnancy.

    Services Include: Free antenatal check-ups, required tests, and counseling by gynecologists/physicians.

  • Mission Indradhanush

    👶 For: Children & pregnant women
    💉 Focus: Complete immunization

    Aims to immunize all children under the age of 2 years and pregnant women against preventable diseases.

    Vaccines Provided: Protection against 12 Vaccine-Preventable Diseases including diphtheria, whooping cough, tetanus, polio, tuberculosis, measles, etc.

  • Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)

    👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For: BPL families
    💰 Coverage: Up to ₹30,000 per family per annum

    A health insurance scheme for the Below Poverty Line (BPL) families with the aim to reduce out-of-pocket expenditure on healthcare.

    Smart Card: Beneficiaries are issued a smart card for cashless treatment at the network hospitals.
